Biased!

As explained in this course, one always needs to be critical with the information they receive when engaging with AI systems. AI tools like Gemini or ChatGPT are always at risk of generating biased responses.

Information becomes biased when it tells you only one side of the story rather than presenting an objective, balanced view. Biased content is often full of stereotypes or leaves out crucial facts about a particular context.

In this task, you'll practice evaluating an AI chatbot's output for bias. Let's get started.

Scenario

Scenario

You are a university student and tasked to make a presentation about Uzbekistan to your international friends. To make your presentation interesting, you want to include some funny facts about the country.

You decide to use ChatGPT to come up with ideas. However, you also need to ensure that anything you share with the audience about Uzbekistan truly represents its culture. After all, you don't want to distort the people's view of the country.

This might be a bit tough task for you, as AI systems are sometimes good at generating content that is, for example, full of stereotypes. Let's see if you can handle this!
